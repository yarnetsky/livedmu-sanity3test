Lived Experiences Through Storytelling

Lived Experiences Through Storytelling: Race at Miami University is a collaborative storytelling project that chronicles the history of racial dynamics at Miami University through documentaries, oral histories, and archival stories of students, alumni, faculty, and staff of color. At the heart of the project are the lived experiences of the past and present who have shared their experiences while providing resources for teaching, studying and learning.
